RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a musical collaboration by Chris Milk, Danger Mouse, etc., and “3 Dreams of Black” is an interactive music film created by them. As the word “interactive” shows, it is totally different from conventional and traditional films/videos in that viewers can freely steer themselves and change what they see as the video goes on. The video consists of three parts (specifically three different dreams), and we can enjoy different experiences along with background music and beautiful graphics. The video itself had a significant meaning when it first appeared around 2011 since it showed the tremendous potential of WebGL in graphics on the web. It is said to be the first interactive Web-based music film in the world, and therefore it inspired lots of developers to create a new, dynamic, vivid, and interactive web graphics.

Technology: The most important technological feature of this film would be the use of WebGL. Unlike common music videos/films, videos created and operated by WebGL work based on code, and essentially what it does is rendering a bunch of visual models in real-time. As the video shows, we can include many graphical components such as 2D models, 3D models, and animation. Here, it is also worth mentioning that the WebGL is an extension of JavaScript. Hence, we can easily see and learn from source code, which enables us to share and develop new ideas from existing code/work. Also, what WebGL requires is only HTML and JavaScript, therefore we can easily enjoy interactive graphical user experiences without any special software, but just with common web browsers such as Google Chrome.
