RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a interactive experience for the song "3 dreams of black". Everywhere I looked/moved the cursor there were animals following/shapes/patterns. This was made 12 years ago. It is real time rendering of visual models."WebGL essentially transforms your browser into a open source video game console." (Gayno). Through the use of WebGL we are able to experience this story-like immersive game. It pushes the boundaries of what the web can do, not just texts and images, but real time graphics,audio,video,etc.

Technology: The technology used is just from our own machine, video card to render graphics. It is like opening up a browser.It is an extension of JavaScript and runs in the browser without any plugins.
