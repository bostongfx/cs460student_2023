RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is an interactive film by Chris Milk . "3 Dreams Of Black" takes the protagonist through three dream worlds through a combination of rich 2D and interactive 3D sequences with great music. The primary impact was in showcasing the creative potential of WebGL, a technology that enables hardware-accelerated 3D graphics in web browsers without the need for plugins.

The technologies used are WebGL a java script library along with HTML 5 and THREE.js
