RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

The interactive film/music video known as RO.ME is undoubtedly creative and ground-breaking. It was created as a joint effort by the band Arcade Fire, Google Creative Lab, and director Chris Milk. The goal of the project was to push the limits of what was possible using web technologies like WebGL and HTML5. The project utilized a wide range of visual effects that responded to user interactions. Hovering the mouse cursor would reveal hidden elements and trigger various effects, making the experience visually engaging and immersive. In 2011, this project showcased the potential of emerging web technologies. It demonstrated that the web could be a powerful platform for interactive storytelling and artistic expression.

Technology: HTML, CSS, Javasacript, WebGL
